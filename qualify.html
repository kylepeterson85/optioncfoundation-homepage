Who May Qualify

  • Adults in the United States
  • People with a disability or health limitation
  • People with a prior felony conviction
  • Long-term unemployed or underemployed adults
  • Motivated to pursue small business ownership

This Program Is

Free for qualified participants
Remote — work from anywhere in the U.S.
Focused on lawful small businesses
Not a loan or investment program
Not a guaranteed income source
