Why Option C Exists

Capable People Are Being Left Out of the Economy

For adults living with disabilities, prior felony convictions, long-term unemployment, or limited access to opportunity, the traditional job market often feels closed. Many of these individuals have skills, experience, and the work ethic to build something real — what they're missing is structure, guidance, and a fair shot. Option C Foundation was created to give them that fair shot through entrepreneurship — not by handing out a workbook and wishing them luck, but by actually building the business with them, side by side.

The Door Keeps Closing

Background checks, gaps in work history, and disability-related limitations cause many capable adults to be screened out before they're ever given a chance to interview, compete, or contribute.

Information Without Implementation

There are plenty of free books, YouTube videos, and entrepreneurship classes — but information alone rarely turns into a launched business. What people actually need is someone to do the work alongside them.

A Different Way Forward

Option C provides a structured, hands-on path. Participants don't have to figure it out alone, hire freelancers they can't afford, or navigate dozens of platforms. We do the heavy lifting while teaching them how everything works.

Our Framework

The System Behind Every Business We Build: The Business Building Machine

The reason Option C can deliver a complete launch package — not just advice — is that everything we do runs on a structured, repeatable framework called the Business Building Machine. It's a 6-stage operating system designed to take a person from "I have an idea" (or no idea at all) to "I have a real, functioning small business with customers."

Every participant moves through the same six stages, in the same order. Each stage has clear inputs, defined outputs, and a checklist of what must be true before moving to the next one. That structure is what makes the difference between people who "try something" and people who actually launch.

The framework was developed by Option C founder Kyle K. Peterson — a multi-business entrepreneur — and is the same operating system used to build real businesses in the real world. It's now available, free, to qualified participants. One stage at a time. No skipping. No guessing.

What Option C Actually Does for You

Inside Each Phase — The Real Work We Do With You

Here's what's happening behind the scenes during each stage of the Business Building Machine. This is the real work — what your mentor and the Option C team are actively doing alongside you, so you're never staring at a blank page wondering what to do next.

Stage 0 — Research & Direction

We Validate Your Idea Before We Build Anything

  • Complete an intake assessment to understand your goals, skills, and constraints
  • Research your market, your competitors, and real customer demand
  • Identify the strongest direction for your specific situation
  • Deliver a written Business Direction Report you approve before moving forward
Stage 1 — Strategic Foundation

We Give Your Business a Clear Identity

  • Define your mission, niche, target customer, and the specific problem you solve
  • Write your positioning — why someone chooses you over the alternative
  • Set a realistic 90-day goal so you know exactly what success looks like
  • Produce your 20+ page custom business plan based on the foundation
Stage 2 — Offer & Path

We Turn Strategy Into a Real Offer

  • Define exactly what you're selling, what's included, and what the customer gets
  • Set your pricing and one clear primary call-to-action
  • Map the customer journey from first contact to delivery
  • Design your logo and core brand visuals around the offer
Stage 3 — Visibility Engine

We Build Your Online Presence — Top to Bottom

  • Build your custom website with copy written for your real audience
  • Apply SEO on every page so customers can find you on Google
  • Set up your Google Business Profile for local visibility
  • Create your Facebook & Instagram business pages
  • Install Google Analytics so we can see what's working
  • Provide free hosting so your site stays live with no monthly bill
Stage 4 — Delivery System

We Help You Actually Deliver — Consistently

  • Document a simple delivery process from sale to completion
  • Set up the basic systems: payments, communication, follow-up
  • Create checklists so each customer gets the same quality experience
  • Walk you through how to handle the first real customers in real time
Stage 5 — Optimize & Grow

We Help You Improve and Move Forward

  • Review the numbers — visitors, leads, sales, what's working, what's not
  • Identify the single biggest bottleneck and fix that one first
  • Provide ongoing 1-on-1 mentorship as you build momentum
  • Help you plan responsibly for the next phase of growth
