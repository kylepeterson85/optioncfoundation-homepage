Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Option C Foundation is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone — including people with disabilities. Accessibility is not an afterthought for us; it is central to our mission.

Our Commitment

Option C Foundation serves adults with disabilities, and we recognize that inaccessible web experiences create real barriers to participation. We are actively working to ensure that our website meets or exceeds the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level.

We believe everyone deserves equal access to information about our programs and services — regardless of the technology they use or the abilities they have.

What We Are Doing

Our current accessibility efforts include:

  • Using semantic HTML to support screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Providing descriptive alt text for meaningful images
  • Ensuring sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Designing forms with clear labels and error messages
  • Making navigation operable by keyboard without a mouse
  • Writing plain, clear language throughout the site
  • Using readable font sizes and responsive layouts that work on all devices

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas of our website may not yet meet all guidelines. We are a small nonprofit in our early stage and are working to continuously improve. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please let us know so we can address it promptly.

Report an Accessibility Issue

If you experience difficulty using any part of our website, or if you need information in an alternative format, please contact us. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
