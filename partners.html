Who We Work With

Organizations That Serve the Same People We Do

Option C Foundation collaborates with organizations that already work with adults facing employment barriers. If the people you serve are motivated and looking for an alternative path, we may be able to help.

Reentry & Reintegration Programs

Organizations supporting people returning from incarceration — transitional housing, reentry case management, second-chance employers, and parole/probation offices looking for referral resources.

Disability Services & Vocational Rehabilitation

Disability service providers, vocational rehabilitation counselors, independent living centers, and organizations supporting individuals with physical or mental health conditions who face employment barriers.

Workforce Development Organizations

American Job Centers, workforce boards, employment training programs, and other organizations that work with adults struggling to enter or re-enter the workforce through traditional employment channels.

Social Service Agencies

Case managers, social workers, nonprofit service organizations, and community agencies that regularly work with adults facing unemployment, housing instability, or other significant life barriers.

Faith-Based & Community Organizations

Churches, mosques, community centers, and faith-based nonprofits that provide support services to community members facing difficult circumstances and who are looking for partner resources.

Adult Education & Community Colleges

Adult education programs, GED programs, community colleges, and continuing education providers whose students may benefit from entrepreneurship support as an alternative career path.

How It Works

What a Referral Partnership Looks Like

We keep the referral process simple. You don't need a formal agreement to start connecting people with our program.

01

Share the Interest Form

The simplest way to refer someone is to share our interest form link — optioncfoundation.org/qualify — directly with individuals who may be a fit. They apply on their own, and we handle the rest.

02

Warm Referral

For a closer collaboration, you can make a warm referral by emailing us at info@optioncfoundation.org with the individual's name and contact information, and we'll reach out directly to introduce the program.

03

Formal Partnership

Organizations that refer multiple clients regularly can establish a formal referral partnership — including program overview materials, a shared point of contact, and periodic updates on outcomes.

Referral Criteria

Who Is a Good Referral Candidate

When considering whether to refer someone to Option C, here's what we look for in candidates.

Good Referral Fit

  • Adult (18+) in the United States facing employment barriers
  • Motivated and genuinely interested in entrepreneurship
  • Able to participate in a remote/virtual program
  • Interested in building a legitimate, lawful small business
  • Willing to commit time and effort to the process

Not the Right Fit

  • Looking primarily for funding, loans, or grants
  • Interested in MLM, pyramid, or passive income schemes
  • Not genuinely interested in entrepreneurship
  • Not located in the United States
  • Pursuing illegal business activities of any kind
